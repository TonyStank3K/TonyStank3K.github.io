About The Yellowstone Supervolcano

Yellowstone Supervolcano, located primarily in the U.S. state of Wyoming, is one of the largest active volcanic systems in the world. Yellowstone also averages between 1,000 and 3,000 earthquakes a year. Most are virtually unnoticeable, with a magnitude of three or less.

Recent Eruption

The most recent major eruption of Yellowstone occurred approximately 640,000 years ago. Since then, there have been smaller volcanic events and geothermal activity in the area.

Next Eruption Prediction

Scientists continuously monitor Yellowstone for signs of potential eruptions. “There is no current activity that is going on that would indicate anything is happening.
